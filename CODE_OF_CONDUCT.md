# Code of Conduct

donto follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Short version

- Be kind.
- Argue ideas, not people.
- Assume good faith. Repair quickly when you've broken trust.
- Harassment of any kind is not tolerated.

## Reporting

Email `conduct@donto.dev` (or the email listed in `Cargo.toml`'s
`authors` field). Reports are confidential. We will respond within
seven days.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, temporarily ban, or permanently ban contributors
whose behavior violates this Code of Conduct. There is no appeal of a
permanent ban for harassment.
